ANNA SHAFFER

Owner, Certified Picture Framer

Anna Shaffer learned framing from Jane Dosset when she purchased her framing business and has been a Certified Picture Framer since 2008, meeting international standards for knowledge in the field and maintaining certification through continuing professional education. Her thirty plus years in health care gave her skills in observation, understanding the “customer” and management of details.

She has always had a love of color and texture and has been involved in crafts of all kinds since childhood. She finds most rewarding supporting and encouraging emerging local artists. She has been a member of the Ketchikan Area Arts and Humanities Council Board since 2012.

Brandon Hoyt

Customer Service, Picture Framer

Brandon Hoyt, framer, has worked for Starboard Frames and Gifts since 2011. He loves the craftsmanship involved in producing custom framed pieces and excels in design creation with the customer. He is also a talented musician, trained in classical piano and percussion, and leader of the heavy metal band “Buck Up Little Kamper.”
